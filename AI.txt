Actuators: Woh tools ya organs jo environment mein action perform karte hain.

‚Üí Robot environment ka data collect karta hai.

Actuators: motors, robotic arms, wheels, etc.
‚Üí Robot environment mein action karta hai, jaise move karna, pick & place karna.
